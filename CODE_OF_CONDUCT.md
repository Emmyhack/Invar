# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect our community members to be respectful, inclusive, and collaborative.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** - Treating all contributors with respect and dignity
- **Being inclusive** - Welcoming people of all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives
- **Being helpful** - Assisting others and sharing knowledge generously
- **Being constructive** - Providing feedback that helps improve the project
- **Being honest** - Being truthful about challenges, limitations, and mistakes
- **Being patient** - Taking time to understand others' viewpoints

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or insulting/derogatory comments
- Trolling, flame wars, or deliberately inflammatory messages
- Sharing others' private information without permission
- Any form of bullying or personal attacks
- Spam or commercial solicitation
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior can be reported by contacting the project team at conduct@invar-project.dev.

All complaints will be:
- **Reviewed** - Thoroughly investigated
- **Confidential** - Handled with discretion
- **Fair** - Evaluated objectively
- **Timely** - Addressed promptly

Responses may include:
- Private warning
- Public warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub, Discord, forums, etc.)
- Official project events
- Public representation of the project
